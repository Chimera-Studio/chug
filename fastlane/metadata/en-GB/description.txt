Chug is a perfect party starter drinking game meant to be played with others. You will discover funny, interesting and weird things you didn't know about your friends.

BRIEF NOTES

- more than 700 unique sentences included
- 12 themed sentence sets to choose from (constantly adding more)
- custom mode enabled, you can add your own sentences
- create your custom presets
- random sentence shuffle, no doubles until all of them are pulled out

HOW IT WORKS

The sentence will describe a person and those who are related to it, take a sip. There are over 700 unique sentences across 12 themes and every person is sure to take a sip at least a few times. You can always add more by using the Custom set. In the Custom sentence set, you can either add on top of the other themed sentence sets or start an empty game and write all of your own rules.

It requires no account, no swapping the phone around the room, just a cup of drink and good fun is guaranteed!
